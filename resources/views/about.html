Vision, Mission and Values

Vision, Mission and Values of the Global Health Education Foundation

Vision

Create a more equitable society by addressing disparities in healthcare access by providing broader access to healthcare knowledge and opportunities in healthcare education.

Mission

To improve and create a more equitable society through access to healthcare services by providing access to healthcare knowledge and education.

Values

Integrity- to operate with honesty and transparency in all facets of the Corporation

Collaboration-Work together as Team utilizing, individuals expertise and skills and openly sharing ideas to reach a common goal

Respect-Foster an understanding of diverse populations, beliefs and culture with the goal of enhancing quality of life through access to health and education
